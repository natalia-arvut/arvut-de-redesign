Careers

Become a one-person fiduciary.

We're looking for accountants who want more than a segment in someone else's process — who want to run clients whole, with a “fiduciary in your pocket”.

What that means: you independently look after dozens of companies. Arvut takes on the bookkeeping production — platform and team. Your work is the client: questions, deadlines, trust, relationships.

“A fiduciary in your pocket”

It's your laptop, with everything on it:

  • all your companies — contacts, staff, their clients and suppliers;
  • books and scadenzari (open-items ledgers), tills and banks;
  • documents linked to every entry;
  • reports — at any moment, not once a year.

Everything always with you — wherever you are.

The one-person fiduciary works in the Arvut team: we run our own practice and the production for Swiss fiduciary firms — the system gives you your clients, not the hunt for them.

Who we tend to work well with.

We value people who:

  • Think in systems, processes and data
  • Learn fast and enjoy solving real operational problems
  • Care about precision, reliability and audit-ready work
  • Prefer building tools and automation to repetitive manual tasks
  • Want to grow in a Swiss-standard, compliance-conscious environment

Profiles we love to meet:

  • Accountants ready to look after their own portfolio of companies
  • Accounting graduates with an interest in technology and process automation
  • Operations professionals with strong digital skills (spreadsheets, APIs, workflow tools)
  • Interns and working students — for hands-on experience

What we offer — in plain words.

  • Real responsibility from day one. No artificial “junior tasks”. Real work with clients.
  • Access to modern financial operations and AI-augmented engineering.
  • A small, mission-driven team building a new category of fiduciary services.
  • The chance to make accounting transparent and understandable for businesses — and, in time, beyond.

Three things in one message.

Write to us via the contact form or to hello@arvut.ch:

  • A short intro — who you are, what you want to build or learn
  • CV or LinkedIn
  • One or two examples of something you've built or improved (a script, a workflow, a project, a case — something practical)

We reply to every serious application.

One final note

Arvut is not a “scale at any cost” startup. We hire slowly. We onboard thoroughly. The right fit matters more than filling the role fastest.
