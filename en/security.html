Security

Trust is verifiable.

Four things a conservative buyer would ask us in the first meeting — we answer them before the meeting.

Your clients remain yours.

Non‑poaching is anchored in the contract: Arvut does not work with your end clients, makes them no offers and does not compete with your prices. White‑label is built so that the client sees your fiduciary firm — logo, colours, name. Arvut is invisible to them.

This is not a promise in an e-mail. It is a contract clause.

Data.

Mandate data is hosted in Switzerland. Every mandate is isolated from the others at the architecture level — only those working on a mandate have access to its data. Documents are kept for 10 years in accordance with OR 958f. Access is personal; actions in the system are logged.

Who does the work.

Arvut is a Swiss company. The books are kept by a team of qualified Arvut accountants together with the ABM platform; the control rule of production is the reconciliation of every figure against the source. Behind every mandate stands a specific person, not a ticket queue.

Control that is structural, not declarative.

No declaration goes out without your approval — that is not a courtesy rule but the construction of the process: filing and signature physically stay on the fiduciary's side. Behind every figure you see the source document; the review takes minutes, not days.
