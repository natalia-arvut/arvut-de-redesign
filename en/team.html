Team

The people behind Arvut.

Five members. Decades of combined experience in Swiss banking, enterprise SaaS, multilingual sales, operations, and accounting. Listed in the order our governance places them — president first, founder last.

Heinz Brägger

President · Zürich, Switzerland

Heinz Brägger holds an Executive MBA from the University of Rochester-Bern and has been President of Arvut GmbH since 2024. With 18 years of management experience in Digital Business at a leading Swiss bank, he led a team of over 200 employees and managed a CHF 200 million budget.

Since 2007, Heinz has been an entrepreneur and Equity Partner at Leverage Experts, specialising in executive search, recruitment, and management consulting. He focuses on bridging strategy, corporate finance, M&A, market dynamics, HR, and technology.

His primary goal is to drive organisational change and transformation, leveraging his network and deep industry knowledge to support sustainable growth and organisational health.

Philosophy: “Change & Win.”

Federico Cesconi

Advisory Board Member · Zürich, Switzerland

Federico Cesconi's expertise spans over 20 years in AI-driven innovation, customer experience management, and advanced business analytics. CEO of sandsiv+, previously Director of Business Intelligence at Cablecom. He graduated from Università degli Studi di Milano (Doctor of Law) and University of Wales (MBA).

A visionary leader in enterprise SaaS and fintech, he brings Fortune 500 experience in scaling AI solutions across highly regulated markets, with deep knowledge of Swiss compliance frameworks like FADP and fintech standards.

Focus: development of DAP without ambiguities, Swiss regulations (FADP, fintech), and SaaS scaling for global B2B leaders.

Philosophy: “Transform business through AI innovations.”

Egizia Meyer

Partnerships & Growth · Basel, Switzerland

Egizia Meyer is a multilingual sales and business development professional fluent in German, French, Italian, and English. With over 15 years of B2B experience, she has driven client acquisition, strategic networking, and market expansion across Switzerland and international markets.

At Palette CAD (2011–2023), she led business development and sales optimisation, focusing on customer onboarding, change management, and integration of SaaS solutions. Previously, she managed marketing and branding for Simon Keller AG and contributed multilingual content for Beauty Forum.

Her strengths: strategic acquisition, networking, and cultivating long-term client relationships.

Philosophy: “Don't wait for opportunity — create it.”

Natalia Kovpinetc

HR & Digital Marketing · Lugano, Switzerland

Natalia holds two higher-education degrees in management and finance, with experience in business valuation and commercial real estate, as well as extensive administrative experience.

Additional education includes graphic and web design, zero-code website development, and AI for business automation.

At Arvut, Natalia leads digital marketing, manages employee records, administers the arvut.ch website, and runs the internal corporate portal. She develops corporate identity, graphic materials, and applies AI for process automation.

Alexander Kheylik

Founder · Lugano, Switzerland

A mathematician by training. Graduated from the Saint Petersburg Mining University and the Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology — both with honours. Resident in Lugano, Switzerland since 1993.

Alexander has deep operational experience with Swiss business formation, accounting, and administrative practice. He is the creator and lead developer of ABM (Business Management System), DAP (Digital Accounting Protocol), A∩B, and Quantum Accounting.

His key task is integration: inside the company and with the external world — and across other Arvut team initiatives based on next-generation accounting.

A note on team-page ordering.

We list the team president-first, advisor-second, founder-last. This is a deliberate choice. It signals that Arvut is a governed company, not a one-person startup — and reflects how decisions are actually made here.
