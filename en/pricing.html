Frequent questions about the price.

What counts as one entry?

One record in the general ledger journal. A document with several lines, booked as one record, is one entry. A parallel book does not double the bill.

How is the volume discount applied?

To the actual total volume of all your mandates over the year. Bills are issued at list price; at year-end, if a threshold is reached, the difference is recalculated in your favour (credit note). Reach 5'000 entries — −5% on the whole volume; 10'000 — −10%.

Is there a minimum volume or an implementation fee?

No. CHF 500/year — for a mandate of any size; there is no software rollout on your side — so no fee for one either.

Can we re-bill at our own price?

Yes. The rate for the end client is entirely yours. Arvut does not work with your clients directly — anchored in the contract (non-poaching).
