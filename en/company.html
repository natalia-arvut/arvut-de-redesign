Company

A Swiss company that keeps the books for fiduciaries.

Arvut produces the bookkeeping for Swiss fiduciaries — so they take on more mandates without hiring. The production is ours; the client, the review and the signature are theirs.

Fiduciaries in Switzerland hit one ceiling: to take on more mandates, they would have to hire accountants who aren't on the market. Arvut lifts that ceiling. The Arvut team and the ABM platform run the production of the books — entries, reconciliations, draft returns — while the fiduciary's staff shift to review, advisory and client relationships.

We do not compete with the fiduciary and do not approach its clients. This is fixed by contract (non‑poaching) and by the white‑label design: the end client sees their fiduciary, not us.

What we believe.

The accountant is irreplaceable.

Behind every mandate there is a specific qualified person, not a ticket queue. Automation takes away the routine — judgement, control and responsibility stay with people.

A number without a source does not exist.

Every amount is reconciled against its source document: bank statement, VAT register, payslip. To the rappen.

Control stays with the fiduciary.

No return goes out without its sign-off. This is not a courtesy rule, but the design of the process.

Extensions — to a real need.

We build new capabilities together with partners for a concrete mandate, instead of rolling out a list of promises. What is ready, we show in the pilot on your books.

Team.

The Arvut team: the founder and the accountants who run the mandates, plus the people who build the platform. Behind the result there is always a person.
