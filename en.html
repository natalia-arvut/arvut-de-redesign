How it works

You upload. We produce. You sign.

No software rollout on your side, no retraining the team.

1

You or your clients upload

The mandate's documents — however suits: e-mail inbox, photo, PDF bank statements. The client can upload directly through the portal under your brand. Every document is recognised, linked and archived for 10 years (OR 958f).

2

We produce

The Arvut team and the ABM platform: entries, creditors and debtors, payment matching, VAT return draft, posting payroll from your payslips. With quality checkpoints. Every figure reconciled against the source.

3

You review and sign

Drafts of declarations and reports come to you for approval. Behind every figure, the source document — review takes minutes, not days. Signature, filing, client communication — yours.

Services

Six areas. One price.

Alles enthalten in CHF 500/year per mandate + CHF 1.50 per entry — no modules, no surcharges.

Financial accounting

General ledger with source document, chart of accounts, trial balance. Multi-currency including parallel books (EUR/CHF) at FTA rates. Year-end draft statements — for your review and signature.

Accounts payable & receivable

Capturing incoming and outgoing invoices, due dates and aging, automatic payment matching, credit-note handling. Who owes whom what — at any time.

VAT

Return draft per period: multiple rates, multi-currency in CHF, control by figures. Automatic health check — missing tax, rate deviations, anomalies — before it goes to your signature.

Payroll posting

From your finished payslips: gross/net and social deductions as entries, payouts reconciled to the rappen, closing the payroll accounts for the period.

Documents & receipts

The mandate inbox: forward an e-mail or take a photo — the document is recognised and becomes a draft entry. Each document linked to its entry, kept for 10 years (OR 958f).

Reporting & client portal

Balance sheet and income statement — comparative, multilingual, any time instead of once a year. White-label portal: real-time books, document upload from the phone, payment status — under your brand.
