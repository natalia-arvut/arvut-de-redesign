Why Arvut

Capacity you don't have to hire.

Capacity without new hires

Qualified accountants are nowhere to be found on the market — and that won't change. Arvut takes the production off your team's hands: entries, reconciliations, declaration drafts. Your people review, advise and look after the client relationships. Same team — more mandates.

New services under your brand

Real-time books, a document inbox (e‑mail or photo — and the document becomes a draft entry), a client portal. All under your brand and your logo. Your clients see you, not us.

Precision under your control

Every figure is reconciled against the source: bank statement, VAT register, payslip — to the rappen. Every period goes to you for review and signature. Filing the declarations and signing the reports stay with your fiduciary firm.

Reconciliation against the source

We find what Excel misses.

Reconciliation against the source surfaces what manual and Excel processes miss: double entries, lost VAT, recognition errors — before they reach the return. We run multi-currency for real, with parallel EUR/CHF books, and we reconstruct accounts from the original documents where needed — all reconciled to the rappen.

We don't publish client names — Swiss norm, and that's exactly how white-label works.

How it works

You upload. We produce. You sign.

Declarations are filed by the fiduciary. The numbers are produced and reconciled by Arvut.

1

You or your clients upload

The mandate's documents — however suits: e-mail inbox, photo, PDF bank statements. The client can upload directly through the portal under your brand — taking document collection off your plate too. No software rollout on your side, no retraining the team.

2

We produce

The Arvut team and the ABM platform: entries, creditors and debtors, payment matching, VAT return draft, posting payroll from your payslips. With quality checkpoints.

3

You review and sign

Drafts of declarations and reports come to you for approval. Signature, filing, client communication — yours.

Arvut doesn't replace the fiduciary. It makes it more profitable. More about the process →
